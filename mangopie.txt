1 can pulp
2 pie crust
2 packets of knox gelatin
3/4 cup sugar
1 cup water room temp.
1/2 pint heavy cream

Take water and dissolve gelatin powder, keep mixing it

Open mango can

Put pot on stove

Turn on the stove on medium heat and add mango pulp and sugar

Keep stirring until it starts boiling (takes a while)

Lower flame

Keep for exactly for ten minutes at low heat
Stir occasionally

Turn stove off and let cool completely until at room temperature
	Try putting pot in ice or bowl of cold water to cool down faster

Add heavy cream 

Mix everything thoroughly

Pour into graham cracker crust

Refrigerate for at least 6 hours
